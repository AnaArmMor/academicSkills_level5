Resource: Websites

There are different web pages in which the job offer within the gaming industry shows the skills that companies are looking for, such as computer skills, interpersonal skills, hard skills vs soft skills. Some of these pages are INDEED,TOTALJOBS,CV-LIBRARY ,etc. In these pages you generally have to create an account and add your resume, this gives you the opportunity to search for a job or for companies to see your CV if it is public.

On the MYGOV.SCOT page you can not only find job offers, there are also guides to guide your career, courses, qualifications, apprenticeships, etc.

On the STATISTA website there is a lot of information about statistics related to the gaming industry and the profession of game developer. For example, distribution of game developers by genre in the world from 2014-2021;Percentage of game developers working on selected game genres worldwide as of March 2019, by type of employment.Average salaries in the games industry worldwide as of June 2018, by region.

The website GDC VAULT, which is one of the largest annual gatherings of game developers in the world. The GDC conference features talks, panels, and workshops from leading industry professionals and covers a wide range of topics related to game design, programming, production, and business. The content available on the GDC Vault website includes video recordings of talks and presentations from past GDC conferences, as well as slides and other related resources. The content is organized into categories such as design, programming, art, business, and advocacy, making it easy for users to find information on specific topics.There are also sections with talks and presentations from leading industry experts, who talk about game design, programming, production and business.

The website GAMEDEVELOPER that offers topics related to game development, including design, programming, art, business, features articles, tutorials and interviews with leading industry experts, as well as news and updates on the latest developments in the game. industry. There are also job postings, event calendars, and a directory of game development companies.

The website GAME INDUSTRY CAREER GUIDE is owned and operated by Jason W. Bay, game designer and author of the book "Game Development Careers: Inside Tips for Finding, Landing, and Succeeding in Your Dream Job." The site contains career guides, eBooks, courses, job listings, resume and portfolio tips, and information on industry events and conferences with professionals speaking on various topics such as game design or programming.

Resource: Library and its resources

Julia Dolowicz


This book contains information on seven steps to create a CV. In the first step you include skills and experiences that are relevant to the job you are applying for. In the second step you organize and format the CV with the information you have. The third step focuses on a clear and effective objective. In the fourth step, the author explains how to highlight the most important skills on the resume. The fifth and sixth step you write the experience and how important it is to put references. Finally the seventh step is the revision of the CV.

Lynn Williams


Readymade job search letters : every type of letter for getting the job you want. London: Kogan Page.

The book is organized into thematic sections that include cover letters, follow-up letters, thank you letters, reference letters, and negotiation letters. Each section contains tips and common mistakes to adapt the letters to different situations as each job offer could be different.


Readymade CVs. Kogan Page Publishers.

The book is organized into thematic sections for entry-level", seasoned professionals", freelancers" or even for a career change".

On this FIFE COLLEGE LIBRARIES website, you can view digitized books and documents, including manuscripts, letters, and historical records. The web page search bar makes it easy to find the information you are looking for quickly. In the case of help to write resumes, there are several books that can be consulted and even purchased as ebooks through other companies.
